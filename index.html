Basic pokemon

Eevee

Requirements to evolve

Eevee is a small, mammalian, quadrupedal Pokémon. Eevee is said to have an irregularly shaped genetic structure that is easily influenced by its environment. This allows it to adapt to a variety of habitats by evolving. Eevee can potentially evolve into 8 different evolutions.

Special move

Eevee is the only known Pokémon capable of using the exclusive Z-Move Extreme Evoboost.

Water pokemon

Vaporeon

Requirements to evolve

Vaporeon is a Pokémon that shares physical traits with both aquatic and land animals. It evolves from Eevee when exposed to a Water Stone. It is one of Eevee's final forms.

Special move

Vaporeon's special ability is Water Absorb.
